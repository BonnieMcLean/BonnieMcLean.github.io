Honest Cooking Blog

About

I decided to make this blog as a way to keep track of recipes I've made that turned out well. It's not a professional cooking blog, and it doesn't contain the recipes themselves, only the links to them. However, what it does provide is an honest opinion from a regular person (NOT a cook) about (a) how much effort the dish was to make, and (b) how much reward the dish was to eat. You can then search for dishes to make by their relative effort versus reward.

I am only including dishes on this blog that I would make again, so usually the reward is always greater than or equal to the effort (otherwise, I wouldn't make it again). However, in a few instances the effort may be greater than the reward, but the dish is still included because it was enjoyable to make. This usually only happens with baking.

Why cooking?

I think cooking is the best hobby. Assuming all goes to plan, it provides (fairly) instant gratification, and everyone needs to eat, so why not do it well.

Asian ingredients

Many of the dishes on here contain asian ingredients you may not be able to find in your local supermarket. If you're stuck on where to find them, one of my favourite food blogs provides a list of asian supermarkets worldwide that you can use to search for supermarkets near you.
