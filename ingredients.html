Essential ingredients

This page contains lists of ingredients I think you should always have on hand so that you can make something decent at any time. If you have these in your kitchen, then you can make most of the dishes on this site without having to buy too many ingredients. All of these are ingredients that either keep forever, or that you will go through on a regular basis, so you don't have to worry about them going off.

I've divided the ingredients into the following categories:

  • Seasonings
  • Baking
  • Fresh
  • Frozen
  • Fridge
  • Pantry
