Lemon Drizzle Cake - BETA

effort:😫    reward: 😋😋

source: personal recipe (see below)

comments:

I'm still perfecting the lemon/sugar ratio in the drizzle for this, but here is the basic recipe. It's based on a recipe from my grandma, which is why everything is in ounces.

Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 4 ounces (=115g) butter
  • 6 ounces (=170g) sugar
  • zest of two lemons
  • 8 ounces (=225g) plain flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 generous Tbsp sour cream/yoghurt/milk (optional)

    • For the drizzle:

  • juice of two lemons
  • 1/3 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 tsp ground cardamom (optional)

    • Directions

    1. Preheat the oven to 180 degrees
    2. Cream the butter and sugar with the lemon zest
    3. Beat in the eggs
    4. Whisk the flour and baking powder together in a separate bowl, then fold them in.
    5. Gently fold in the sour cream/yoghurt/milk if adding
    6. Bake for about 45 minutes
    7. As soon as it's out of the oven, and while still in the tin, poke holes in the cake everywhere then pour over the juice of two lemons mixed with the sugar and (if you like) a dash of cardamom
    8. Let it sit in the tin for a while and absorb all the lemon
    9. Once it's cooled, transfer to a wire rack to serve. It should have crispy bits of sugar on top.
