Pavlova

effort: 😫😫    reward: 😋😋😋+

source: Benjamina Ebuehi

comments:

  • I was skeptical about heating the sugar up in the oven, but it really did make a difference in getting the egg whites glossy and smooth.
  • Was also a bit meh about 'mini' pavlovas but actually it's very convenient for leftovers, you don't have to worry about the pavlova getting soggy and can just make it fresh each time.
  • I didn't bother with all the fancy stuff she adds to the cream, think cream tastes good enough as is.
  • This pavlova is with poached rhubarb with a dash of gin, it's amazing but of course any fruit on pavlova is amazing.
