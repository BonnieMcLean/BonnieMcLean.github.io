Golden pudding

effort:😫😫    reward: 😋😋😋

source: Nigella

comments:

  • Most of the effort in this is that you have to steam it for a long time. Making the batter is super easy, you just do it all in a blender and if you put the blender on a set of scales and measure the ingredients straight into the blender you'll have hardly any washing up. Yes, technically, you could just cook this in the oven/microwave in less time and it's *basically* the same BUT nothing beats the texture of real steamed pudding.
  • Goes well together with homemade vanilla icecream!
  • The golden syrup in this is a British/Aussie thing, if you live in Europe (not UK) you'll probably have to get it from some online/import shop. There is nothing outside Australia/NZ/UK that's really as good as golden syrup.
