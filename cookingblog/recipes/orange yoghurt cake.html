Orange Yoghurt Cake

effort: 😫😫    reward: REWARD SCORE

source: personal recipe (see below)

comments:

Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/4 cups sugar
  • 2/3 cup olive oil
  • 1 cup yoghurt
  • zest of two oranges (about 2 Tbsp)
  • juice of one orange (about 1/4 cup)
  • 2 1/4 cups flour
  • 4 tsp baking powder
  • pinch of salt

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 200 degrees celcius.
  2. Whisk together sugar, oil, yoghurt, and orange juice and zest.
  3. Add flour, baking soda and salt and whisk until just combined
  4. Place cake in the oven and immediately turn down to 180, bake for 50 minutes.
