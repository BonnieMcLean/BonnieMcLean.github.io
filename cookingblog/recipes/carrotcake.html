Best Carrot Cake

source: Nagi

comments:

This is the best carrot cake recipe I've found, the crushed pineapple really works (just make sure it's well crushed). The instructions for the icing though are (I think) a bit off. There's no way you need that much icing sugar, just keep adding it to the cream cheese and taste testing; you'll probably only need around 1 cup at most. This also makes way more icing than you need for the cake, but I don't know that you can get cream cheese in smaller packets. If you can, I would try to halve the ingredients for the icing. However, if you can't, the good news is that this frosting freezes really well, so you can just make all the icing and freeze whatever you don't use (that's what I did). The cake, even iced, also freezes perfectly. Just remember to slice it into bite sized pieces before freezing, then you can just take pieces out of the freezer to defrost and get through it a little at a time, because this does make A LOT of cake. But it's totally worth it, and then you can have emergency cake whenever you want!
